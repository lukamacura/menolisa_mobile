# Menolisa mobile illustration prompts

Use these prompts when generating or refreshing **quiz step art**, **mood check-in**, and any new PNGs that should feel part of the same family as `assets/symptoms/*.png`.

## Global style (must match symptom illustrations)

**Soft digital watercolor** with **delicate dark brown (coffee) line art**—hand-drawn feel, thin strokes, not heavy black comic outlines. Translucent washes, wet-on-wet blending, optional subtle paper grain. **Muted warm pastels:** peach, cream, blush pink, pale sky blue, sage / muted green, soft lavender, warm grey; align with app primary coral `#ff8da1` only as a soft accent, not neon. Lighting: soft gradients and gentle glows only—no harsh cel shading or high-contrast digital airbrush. Mood: calm, cozy, supportive health companion. **Transparent background.** Centered subject; for quiz steps use a **square** safe area (e.g. export 1024×1024, important content in the middle).

Append to every subject prompt: *“Menolisa global illustration style; transparent PNG.”*
